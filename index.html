Research Areas

Project A: Advanced AI Modeling

Focusing on deep learning architectures for real-time data processing and predictive analytics. Current work involves reinforcement learning in complex simulation environments.

  • Development of novel spatio-temporal graph models.
  • Application in smart city traffic prediction systems.
  • Collaboration with industry partners on data privacy.

Project B: Sustainable Energy Systems

Investigating new materials and designs for high-efficiency solar cells and energy storage solutions. This includes photochemical and electrochemical studies.

Project C: Computational Biology

Applying large-scale computing to analyze genomic data and protein folding mechanisms. We develop custom algorithms for biomarker discovery.
