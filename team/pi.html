About

Dr. Seug-Jae Shin leads THEMMES group with research interests in multiscale simulation of (photo-)electrochemical systems and materials modelling. He supervises projects across reaction dynamics, disordered materials, and interphase phenomena.

Research Interests

  • Multiscale simulation of electrochemical interfaces
  • Reaction dynamics and kinetics
  • Disordered materials and defects
